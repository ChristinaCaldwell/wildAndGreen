About Us

We are a family owned flower farm based in rural Moyvalley, Co. Kildare. All flowers are grown on site and sold at the farmgate from Spring 2020. It is our aim to provide a sustainable, locally grown product for our value customers.

Local

All our flowers are grown on-site and have zero air miles

Unique

We are passionate about growing speciality flowers and designing with them

Sustainable

We want to contribute to the health of our planet, not deplete it.
